Welcome to my hobby

I'm a father of two kids (born 2015 & 2017) and working full time as an optometrist and hearing aid dispenser in my own business in the beautiful Canton of Glarus in Switzerland.

In the 90's I was specialized in data communication and run the NoName BBS bulletin board system, which was part of the FIDO-Network and a VGA Planets host.

Altough electronics and computers were my profession, they were always also a hobby for me. Today I like playing with Arduino boards - mainly ESP32.

To improve my programming skills I completed online courses in modern C++, HTML, CSS and a few others – but I'm still far from a professional programmer.
